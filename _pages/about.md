I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Learning Technical Systems group at Ruhr University Bochum. Prior to this, I completed my PhD in the Computer Science Department at the University of Tübingen.

My research interests lie at the intersection of:

- Online learning, with a focus on bandit learning.
- Communication systems, with emphasis on edge networks.
- Distributed learning, particularly federated learning and its applications to 5G and future 6G systems.
